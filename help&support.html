HOW TO SUCCEED ON FIVERR: A GUIDE FOR BUYERS

Whether you're a small business owner, solopreneur, or other buyer, this guide will help you get more from your Fiverr experience.

faq

  • Can I pay with my credit card? Yes, you can pay with your credit card or debit card. For more information on payment methods, Paying for Services.
  • What if I'm not happy with my delivery? If you are not happy with your delivery, you can always ask the seller for revisions. You can also resolve any further issues with your order through our Resolution Center.
  • What do the seller levels mean? A seller level is a status earned by sellers based on ratings, performance, quality, and other factors. For more information, see Fiverr's Levels.
  • do I contact the seller before I place my order? Within the Gig page, you will see a "Contact Me" button. We recommend contacting your seller before placing your order, especially if you have specific requirements.
